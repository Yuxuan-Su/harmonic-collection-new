INGREDIENTS

Shepherd's purse, pork stuffing, all-purpose flour, cooking wine, light soy sauce and dark soy sauce, minced green onion, minced ginger, salt, sesame oil, oyster sauce and five-spice powder.

Mama's Pork Elbow Soup

Pressure cooker version

老妈蹄花汤

T THIS DISH IS RECOMMENDED BY MORANDI LI

Mama's Pig trotters soup a famous traditional food in Chengdu, Sichuan Province, and the origin of the name has a long story. She can be served as a soup or eaten straight from the pig's feet with a dipping sauce, both are very tasty. It is a recipe Morandi learned from her mom.

STEP 1: Cut the pig's trotters into cubes and put them into cold water, then add green onion and ginger to blanch.

STEP 2: Bring to a boil and skim off the froth.

STEP 3: Rinse the butter bean from the can with water.

STEP 4: Cut the vegetables (carrots, yams) and put the pig's feet into the pressure cooker.

STEP 5: Add salt and simmer for 40 minutes.

STEP 6: Put the chili noodles, green onions, and garlic in a bowl.

STEP 7: Add one tablespoon of soy sauce and two tablespoons of dark soy sauce and pour hot oil over the top.

STEP 8: You can drink the soup, pig's feet can be used with the dipping sauce just prepared.
